FrameShift Explore

Protoype

Main prototype:FrameShift Explore

Question statment: frameshift tours subject: initial app screen design requirements monday, 12th april 2198 hey designer greetings from the year 2198. we are frameshift, a time travel company. we provide short educational tours across the expanse of time and back. we have battle-tested our time travel device and it works pretty well (that’s kind of how we are here). in the future, ai has taken over and we don’t get good creative designs anymore. we are here, in the golden age of design, to get some screens designed for our app called frameshift explore. explore is like uber but for time travel. you can book short rides to different points in time and back. sound neat, doesn’t it? it’s all made possible via our self-driving time travel pods that you can hail whenever. we are looking to get the following screens designed: the ride-booking screen (to hail a frameshift pod) the screen you see when you’re en route the payment screen we haven’t yet decided how payments are going to work on our app. can you help us out a little? try to make the app as classy as possible please, we are a bunch of scientists and we don’t know how to do that.
